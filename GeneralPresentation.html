1 Welcome to AC Aviation!

1.1 AC Aviation Background

AC Aviation Company established on March 2010 with the capital registration of Thai Baht 44 million (US$1.4million).

The company successfully obtained Thai Aircraft Operator license in 2012 and started its operations in the same year with two Beechjet aircrafts of Thai registration HS-ASC & HS-BRM. In April 2013, AC Aviation accepted a Hawker 850XP aircraft of HS-PEK to join its 3rd aircraft into the fleet. The flight services have always been charters and air ambulance (Medevac) within the Asian region.
